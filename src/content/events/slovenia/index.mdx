## Europe's hidden green heart

Cross borders on two wheels through Europe's best-kept secret. The Soča guides you
along emerald water for days. Ancient forests whisper stories only cyclists get to
hear. Vršič lifts you above the treeline at dawn. And somewhere between Bovec and
Kobarid you realize you've crossed into Italy and back without noticing — because
on a bike, borders are only lines on a map.

**550 kilometres through three countries and one of the quietest corners of the Alps.**
