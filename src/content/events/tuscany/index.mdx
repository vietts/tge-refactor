## Italy, distilled

Rolling hills dotted with cypress trees. Medieval towns that crown every ridge.
Renaissance cities where art lives in the streets, not just museums. The white
gravel roads of the *strade bianche*, the same ones the pros race in March — but
you'll ride them without a number on your back.

**600 kilometres from Siena to Siena, through the Italy everyone dreams about.**
